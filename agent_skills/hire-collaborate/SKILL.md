## How to write a good outreach

**Bad:** "Hey, would love to chat about an opportunity."

**Good:** "I'm building a Rails 8 app with SQLite for a CRM-style product, and I'm hitting a wall on background-job patterns now that we're moving away from Sidekiq. Saw your post on simplifying Causey — could I get 20 minutes to compare notes?"

The second one gets a yes. The first one gets archived.
